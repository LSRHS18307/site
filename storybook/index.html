The Story of the Missing Laptop

Meet Jack, Jack is 25 years old and has recently obtained a job working for the CDC. His Job has long hours and no vacation. Currently he takes the train into work every day and works on his computer while on the train. One day when he was working on his computer he was hungry and decided to go and get an English muffin. When he returned from the food bar his laptop was gone. He looks everywhere for it but he can not find. On the laptop there is top secret information about the ebola virus which is currently a huge threat to the west.
